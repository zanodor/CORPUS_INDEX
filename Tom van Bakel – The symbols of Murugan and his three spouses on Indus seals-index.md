However, in the Indus Valley Civilization, among other things, Murugan was depicted as a compound animal composed of six other animals, and he had three women instead of two: Devasena, Valli, and Shashthi, who were completely equal to each other. He was indeed identified with Indra, Varuna and Vishnu, but the fourth god was Surya instead of Kali.
