Konkan petroglyphs are Indus Script hieroglyphs of
metalwork wealth resources of Tin-Bronze Revolution
Some petroglyphs of Konkan appear to be remembered hieroglyphs of Sarasvati-Sindhu Civilization -- all the hieroglyphs signify metalwork wealth of Tin-Bronze Revolution from 4th millennium BCE..
1. karibha, ibha 'elephant' rebus: karba, ib 'iron' 2. med 'body' rebus: med 'iron' 3. aya 'fish' rebus: aya 'iron' ayas 'alloy metal'
4. khambhaṛā 'fish-fin' rebus: kammaṭa 'coinage, coiner, mint' 5. kharā 'hare' rebus: khār 'blacksmith'
6. Ta. araṇai typical lizard, Lacertidae; smooth streaked lizard, Lacerta interpunctula. Ma. araṇa green house lizard, L. interpunctula. Ka. araṇe, rāṇe, rāṇi greenish kind o lizard which is said to poison by licking, L. interpunctula.
