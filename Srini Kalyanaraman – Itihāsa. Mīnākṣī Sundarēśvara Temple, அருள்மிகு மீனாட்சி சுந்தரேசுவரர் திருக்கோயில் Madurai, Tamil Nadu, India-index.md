திருக்ரகாயில் . Mīnākṣī Sundarēśvara Temple. Mentioned in 6th-century-CE texts of Sangam literature. West tower (gopuram) of the temple is the model based on which the Tamil Nadu State Emblem is designed. Built by King Kulaśekhara Pāṇḍya (1190–1216 CE).
