-- Deśi means ‘guild of merchants’
--Deśī (देशी) is one of the varieties of the Prākṛta dialect; see Kāv.1.33.
-- Hieroglyphs of Indus Script Corpora signify Deśī words of Mleccha, Meluhha
-- Symbols on ancient Indian coins signify Deśī words of Mleccha, Meluhha
– Technical expressions used by linguists for this feature are: Indian sprachbund, ‘speech union’ or
‘linguistic area’
