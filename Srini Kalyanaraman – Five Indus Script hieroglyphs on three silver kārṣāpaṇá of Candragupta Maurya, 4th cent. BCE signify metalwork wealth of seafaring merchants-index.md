This is an addendum to:Ten Indus Script hieroglyphs on two silver kārṣāpaṇá of Candragupta Maurya, 4th cent. BCE document mint wealth https://tinyurl.com/6fjh4mp9
The following set of three three silver kārṣāpaṇá of Candragupta Maurya, 4th cent. BCE (GH 512, GH 516, GH 519) evidence five hieroglyphs which are deciphered and read in Meluhha rebus of Indus Script Cipher.
Hieroglyphs 1 to 5 are as shown on the two silver kārṣāpaṇá https://tinyurl.com/6fjh4mp9
