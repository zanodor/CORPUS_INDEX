# Index for Srini Kalyanaraman – Temple of Sarasvati Sindhu Civilization identified message from temple deciphered.pdf

Temple of Sarasvati-Sindhu Civilization
identified; message from temple deciphered
-- Signs 242, 243 are signifiers of a two-tier building with fire-altar, kole.l
‘temple’
-- Signified is kole.l ‘smithy, forge’
-- The message of the temple is for कटकम् kaṭakam a caravan of metalsmiths
and maritime seafaring merchants
-- The caravan venerated pōtṛ or potṛ, ‘Purifier’, N. of one of the 16
officiating priests at a sacrifice (the assistant of the Brāhman; = yajñasya
śodhayiṭri, Sāy. ), RV.
-- Tradition of Cosmic Dancer metaphor continues into historical periods
