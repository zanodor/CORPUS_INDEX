Eastern Iran seal of Ligabue Collection is in Indus Script signifies metalwork trade
Ligabue Collection has a (said to be) Linear Elamite text on this seal, signifying East Iranian participation in the trading sphere between Meluhha and Ancient Near East.. Stamp seal in Indus style Eastern Iran or Oxus Late III millennium BC Steatite with modern impression: a bull, and (said to be) proto-elamite script Ligabue Collection, Venice, inv.
