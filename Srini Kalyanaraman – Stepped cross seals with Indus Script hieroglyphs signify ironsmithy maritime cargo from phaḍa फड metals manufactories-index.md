Malhar yajnakunda Malhar is an archaeological site on the Ganga River Basin in the Dist. of Bilaspur. At this site, a yajna kunda, a sacred fire altar has been discovered, dated ca. 1900 BCE, a date when iron was smelted in a number of furnaces discovered in sites of Ganga basin such as Lohardiwa, Raja-Nal-ki-Tila,in addition to Malhar.
