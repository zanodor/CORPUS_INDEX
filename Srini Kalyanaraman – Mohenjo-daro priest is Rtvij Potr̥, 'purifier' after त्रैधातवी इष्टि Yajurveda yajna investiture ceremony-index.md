Mohenjo-daro priest is Rtvij Pot ̥r, 'purifier' after ैत्रधातवी
इष्टि Yajurveda yajna investiture ceremony
--This monograph provides archaeological and textual evidences to prove that the Mohenjo-daro priest is Pot ̥r, 'purifier priest' of Yajurveda (Taittiriya) following the procedures detailed in शतपथ-ब्राह्मण |
--Details of ैत्रधातवी इष्टि provide for attainment of three minerals, copper, silver, gold through yajna. Trefoil on priest’s garment is an investiture ceremony after this yajna.
-- The Mohenjo-daro priest statue was discovered in an archaeological stratigraphy dated to ca. 2500 BCE; the statue was found in the 'temple' which also produced a cache of 12 Indus Script seals.
