Signifiers: 8 ṛtvij, Perforated jars and
basket of metal ingots
I submit that the perforated jars contained alkaline material to
oxidise the impurities in ores being smelted; the perforations also
allowed godhuma ‘wheat chaff’ fumesto infuse carbon element
into molten infusions to harden iron into crucible steel and
produce hard cast iron ingots. This process of godhuma fumes is
described in Vedic texts.
